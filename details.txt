Installing virtual environment

1. Test your instalation:
$ pipenv --version

2. Naming Vitual Env:
$ python -m nenv room1

3. Activeing Virtual Env:
$ <venv>\Scripts\active.bat

4. Deactivating Virtual Env:
$ deactivate
